# Pure Drop Reports

**Agent:** Tine (Head of Pure Drop)  
**Purpose:** Research repository for Pure Drop product development

## What This Repo Is For

This repository contains research reports, market analysis, and strategic recommendations for **Pure Drop** - a creatine supplement in a shot-bottle format with a powder-release cap.

### Reports Stored Here

- Product viability studies (demand, positioning, differentiation)
- Supply chain research (manufacturers, COGS, sourcing options)
- Market analysis (competitors, pricing, target segments)
- Strategic recommendations (GO/NO-GO decisions)

### Agent Responsibilities

**Tine** coordinates specialist research across:
- **Product Specialist** - Market demand & product positioning
- **Supply Chain Specialist** - Manufacturing & cost analysis
- **Brand Marketing Specialist** - Marketing strategy & positioning
