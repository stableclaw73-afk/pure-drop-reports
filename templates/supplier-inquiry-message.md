## Tips for Sending

1. **Personalize Each Message:**
   - Insert the actual product name from the supplier's listing
   - Reference their specific features (e.g., "Your PCO1810 standard threading")

2. **Attach Photos (if available):**
   - Target bottle you'll be using
   - Creatine powder reference
   - Competitive product examples

3. **Follow Up:**
   - If no response in 48 hours, send a short follow-up
   - Alibaba suppliers respond faster to multiple inquiries

4. **Priority Order:**
   - **Day 1:** Contact #11 (in-stock sample), #1, #2
   - **Day 2:** Contact #4, #5
   - **Day 3:** Contact #8, #9, #3

5. **Track Responses:**
   - Create a spreadsheet with supplier responses
   - Compare quotes, lead times, and minimum orders

## What to Expect

**Response Time:** 12-48 hours (Chinese business hours)  
**Common Questions from Suppliers:**
- Exact bottle dimensions/threading
- Annual volume projections
- Target price point
- Delivery timeline

**Red Flags:**
- Suppliers asking for payment before samples
- Unwilling to provide certifications
- Vague on lead times or MOQs

**Green Flags:**
- Quick responses with detailed specs
- Willing to send samples before committing
- Provide technical drawings and certificates
- Ask intelligent questions about your application

5. **Place Test Order** (1,000-1,500 pieces) with best 2 suppliers
6. **Scale Production** based on test results
